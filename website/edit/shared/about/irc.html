IRC

Why IRC ?

One way that Mono developers and users try to help support each other is through a group chat room that is on Internet chat rooms (IRC). This allows for what is otherwise known as real time interaction. Everyone chats in a room, called a channel. You can ask any questions you want, and for the most part, they will be answered.

IRC clients

IRC clients are many. We prefer xchat on Linux, Colloquy on MacOS X and mIRC on Windows but your mileage may vary.
