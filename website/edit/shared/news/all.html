04 May 2004: Mono 1.0 Beta 1 released (Permalink)

It's on ! This is one of the two betas for Mono that are planned before our 1.0 release.
The major highligths of this release are:

  • SPARC JIT engine
  • Windows.Forms is working again (alpha preview)
  • Native asynchronous support has been added to our I/O layer.
  • Relax NG compact syntax parser.
  • FileSystemWatcher.
  • ADO.NET disconnected operation.
