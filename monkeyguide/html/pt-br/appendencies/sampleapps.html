The best way to learn something new is often to look at samples. The more the better.
This tutorial has hundreds of samples (or will have at least), but there are some real applications available, that are worth a look.

MPhoto

  • Application for managing you digital photos (only in cvs)

Phonic

  • Gtk# audio Player, makes use of Gstreamer

Platano Multimedia Player

  • Gtk# video/audio player, makes use of GStreamer

GSIRC

  • Gtk# cross platform IRC client

GnoMencoder

  • DVD ripping utility

PolarViewer

  • A viewer for exercise files recorded with Polar heartrate monitors.

Jump n Bump menu

  • A Graphical frontend to Jump n Bump

Bomb O Naval

  • A classic board game, where you will fight a great sea battle.
