Monekyguide aims to target:

  • people coming from Windows, who know .NET and want to get known to the mono tools
  • people coming from Windows, who want to program Gnome 2 applications
  • people switching from another language (C, Java, etc.)
  • newbie programmers, that want to learn coding

No matter how much you already know, this guide is for you. Experienced people can always skip boring chapters.
